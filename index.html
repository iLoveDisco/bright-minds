Math Program

Our math program is designed to achieve mastery of computational skills leading to success in high school and college math. Mastery equals confidence speed and accuracy in our program. Materials are arranged sequentially from the beginning number sense through high school algebra and geometry. Our individualized pacing benefits children of all ages and abilities. Each concept is practiced until real mastery is achieved. Through the daily and practice students advance in small manageable increments while improving their concentration, developing better study skills and increasing their confidence.
